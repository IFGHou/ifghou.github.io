FAQ

How to I change my email address on record?

To change your email address for InfraGard correspondence, you must contact InfraGard Technical Support 24x7.
This is the only method to get your email address changed. No one in the Houston IMA chapter or the FBI Coordinator has access to make the change.
Domestic (877)861-6298
International (225)334-5952
Help Desk infragardhelpdesk@leo.gov

How often should I get an InfraGard email?

An email is sent from the InfraGard listserve once per month to verify your email address. If you do not receive one, you should call the helpdesk number above and verify your information.
Other emails may be sent as conditions warrant, or when deemed appropriate. Members should check the website for the latest information regarding the chapter, all announcements will be posted on the webstie, Twitter, Facebook, and email, in that order. Announcements made in Twitter and Facebook via the blogs won't be emailed.

How do I receive my ID cards?

Membership ID Cards are distributed when members receive their welcome packets. On a regular basis all vetted members are provided with new cards, they are shipped directly to the InfraGard Coordinator.
In order to obtain your ID card by mail, you must send a self-addressed stamped envelope to FBI coordinator listed on the Officers and Board tab.
